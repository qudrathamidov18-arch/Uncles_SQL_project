# 🏬 MallMaster: Final Project Summary

## 📝 Project Overview
**MallMaster** is a comprehensive Shopping Mall Management System designed to streamline product inventory, sales processing, and user management. The goal was to create a reliable, fast, and user-friendly tool for cashiers, store managers, and mall admins.

## 🚀 Key Accomplishments

### 1. Process Optimization (Week 1 & 2)
Mapped out the **BPMN (Business Process Model and Notation)** to ensure every sale and restock follows a logical flow, reducing errors and improving checkout speed.

### 2. Functional Requirements (Week 3)
Defined the **Use Case Diagram** for main actors:
* **Mall Admin:** System control, reporting, and store oversight.
* **Store Manager:** Inventory management and sales monitoring.
* **Cashier:** Fast checkout and product search.

### 3. Robust Database Design (Week 4)
Developed a relational **ERD (Entity Relationship Diagram)** with a 3rd Normal Form (3NF) structure.
* **Tables:** Users, Products, Sales, and Suppliers.
* **Themes:** Dark Blue (Security & Admin functions) & Gold (Inventory & Actions).

### 4. Professional UI/UX (Week 5)
Designed a high-contrast **Wireframe** focusing on speed, clarity, and accessibility.
* **Colors:** Dark Blue (#0D47A1) and Gold (#FFC107).  
* **Feature:** 1-click checkout system with instant inventory updates.

## 🎯 Final Conclusion
The project successfully bridges the gap between complex mall inventory management and simple daily retail operations. **MallMaster** is now ready for development and deployment.
